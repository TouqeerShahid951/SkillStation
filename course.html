Intermediate

This degree represents a significant step forward, marking your progression from foundational knowledge to a deeper understanding of your chosen field. As you embark on this phase, you will delve into more specialized subjects, expanding your expertise and cultivating critical thinking skills.

Graduation

This degree represents the pinnacle of your undergraduate studies, encapsulating the depth of knowledge and skills you have acquired throughout your time in higher education. As you embark on this final phase, you are prepared to dive even deeper into your chosen field, exploring advanced concepts and engaging in rigorous intellectual discourse.

The post-graduation level degree is designed to push the boundaries of knowledge, fostering innovation, and preparing you to become a leader and a trailblazer in your field. This degree opens doors to a world of exciting opportunities, ranging from influential positions in academia to impactful roles in industry and beyond.

General Eligibility:

Sixteen years of schooling or 4 years education after HSSC/A’ Level in relevant discipline with minimum CGPA of 2.0 out of 4.0 OR 55% marks (percentage will only be considered if CGPA is not mentioned on the transcript).

GAT (General) conducted on or after 01 May 2021 till May 2023 by NTS with at least 50 accumulative test score or Higher Education Aptitude Test (HAT) conducted by HEC in or after May 2022 till May 2023 with at least 50 accumulative test score or GRE (General) conducted by ETS, USA, with following minimum score separately in each section:-

Bachelors/Masters degree must be accredited by PEC/HEC (as applicable) in relevant disciplines as mentioned below:-
